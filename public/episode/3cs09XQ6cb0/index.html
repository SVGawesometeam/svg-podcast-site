About the Host

Marina Mogilko
Host, Silicon Valley Girl Podcast

Entrepreneur, content creator, and founder based in Silicon Valley. Marina interviews the world's top tech leaders, investors, and innovators to uncover the trends, strategies, and mindsets shaping the future. With millions of followers across platforms, she brings a unique perspective on technology, business, and personal growth.

In this episode of the Silicon Valley Girl Podcast, Marina Mogilko shares Marina Mogilko analyzes data from over 30 major research reports and insider interviews to identify the top high-paying careers that AI cannot replace. She introduces the EPOCH Framework of five human superpowers, outlines hybrid roles combining human and AI skills, and presents a 3-step strategy for future-proofing your career in 2025. The episode also covers the resurgence of skilled trades and how workers with AI skills now command a 56% wage premium.

Key Takeaways

  • Workers with AI skills command a 56% wage premium in 2025, up from 25% the previous year — making AI literacy one of the highest-ROI skills to develop now.
  • Industries most exposed to AI are seeing 3x higher revenue per employee growth than those least exposed, according to PWC's analysis of nearly 800 million job postings.
  • The World Economic Forum projects 11 million new AI-related jobs even as 40% of employers plan some workforce reductions — net job creation is still positive.
  • MIT research shows most jobs are not economically beneficial to automate right now because hiring humans remains cheaper, slowing mass displacement.
  • Marina introduces the EPOCH Framework — five uniquely human superpowers AI cannot replicate — as the foundation for identifying careers safe from automation.
