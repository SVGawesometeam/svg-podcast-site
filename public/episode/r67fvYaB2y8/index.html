Key Takeaways

  • Claude AI can autonomously control your computer — navigating apps and executing tasks without manual input, representing a major leap in AI-driven automation.
  • Rewind AI acts as a personal memory assistant by recording and indexing everything on your device, giving users instant recall of past conversations, meetings, and browsing activity.
  • Palette.fm uses AI to add realistic color to black-and-white photos, making it a powerful tool for photographers, historians, and content creators with minimal effort.
  • Pika Art is a viral content creation tool that generates short-form AI video, helping creators produce engaging visual content quickly for social media platforms.
  • TimeHero is an AI-powered task manager that automatically schedules your to-do list around your calendar, helping users complete projects on time without manual planning.
