  • The generative AI market is projected to reach between $200 billion and $1.3 trillion by 2030 with a 25% growth rate — making now the ideal time to launch a niche AI product.
  • Building a 'Content Factory' service is one of the most in-demand opportunities right now, as companies and creators need to publish across LinkedIn, YouTube, newsletters, Reels, and more without large teams.
  • Tools like Poppy AI, InVideo, ElevenLabs, and EasyGen allow solopreneurs to automate content creation end-to-end with no prior technical experience.
  • Large companies like Google, Microsoft, and Amazon are moving fast but cannot solve niche problems — that gap is where individual founders can build and win.
  • In 2025, entrepreneurs with no coding background can go from idea to MVP in as little as 24 hours using tools like Replit, Lovable, and Cursor.
