Key Takeaways

  • Email marketing drives 25% of total sales across Marina's businesses, with 700+ newsletters sent per month — proof that consistent volume compounds into significant revenue.
  • Lead magnets are the most effective list-building tool: offer a free, high-value resource (checklist, guide, top-programs list) that sparks interest in your product without fully solving the problem.
  • Email lists are a true owned asset — unlike social media followers where only ~10% see posts due to algorithm changes, an email sent is guaranteed to reach the recipient's inbox.
  • If you stop sending emails for 6 months, platforms can revoke access to your list — the rule of thumb is to show up regularly or download and save your list before any planned break.
  • List hygiene matters: segmenting your audience and regularly cleaning inactive subscribers protects deliverability and keeps your open rates and ROI high.

Analyze and optimize: always have numbers. If you don't have any numbers, if you don't look at the numbers, you can't control anything. And to enhance performance, we frequently conduct A/B tests. A/B testing lets you compare two versions of an email to see which performs better. For example, you're changing the subject line. Or you're changing the call-to-action button. Or you're changing the text. Like, in this email, you were selling the product by describing its features. Based on your analysis, optimize your newsletters. Subject lines: maybe you need an emoji, maybe you don't. Do you need a question mark or not? Do you need urgency or not?
