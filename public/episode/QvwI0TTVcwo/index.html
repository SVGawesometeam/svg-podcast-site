About the Guest

a special guest

In this episode of the Silicon Valley Girl Podcast, Marina Mogilko interviews a special guest, . On her 35th birthday, Marina Mogilko shares 10 actionable life lessons she wishes she had known in her 20s. Drawing from personal experience — including regrets about not investing earlier, neglecting nutrition, and holding back on big dreams — she offers candid advice to help young people maximize their potential before responsibilities multiply. Topics range from working hard and living in exciting cities to addressing mental health early and starting to invest with as little as $20.

Key Takeaways

  • Work like crazy in your 20s — you have more energy and fewer responsibilities, making it the best time to experiment, fail, and learn without major consequences.
  • Start investing even with just $20 — Marina regrets spending early income on luxury goods instead of building passive income that could have supported early retirement.
  • Live in a challenging, exciting city before 30 — costs like private school and family logistics make moves to cities like New York far harder once children enter the picture.
  • Chase your wildest dreams now — Marina didn't pursue singing or acting early because she didn't feel 'ready,' but readiness is self-defined and waiting only adds more mental barriers.
  • Prioritize nutrition and health check-ups early — Marina's cholesterol levels suffered from years of neglect, and she notes it's far easier to build healthy habits in your 20s than to fix problems at 35.
