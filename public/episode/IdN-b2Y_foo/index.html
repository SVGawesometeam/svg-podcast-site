About the Guest

Marie Forleo
Entrepreneur, Author, and Business Coach; Founder of MarieTV and B-School

Marie Forleo is a world-renowned entrepreneur, author of the New York Times bestseller 'Everything is Figureoutable,' and creator of MarieTV and B-School, an online business training program with students in over 170 countries. She began her career on the floor of the New York Stock Exchange before leaving to build a global coaching and media brand. Forleo has been recognized by Oprah Winfrey as a thought leader for the next generation and is known for her philosophy of combining business strategy with personal alignment.

In this episode of the Silicon Valley Girl Podcast, Marina Mogilko interviews Marie Forleo, Entrepreneur, Author, and Business Coach; Founder of MarieTV and B-School. Marina Mogilko interviews Marie Forleo about the principles behind making faster, more aligned decisions in life and business. Marie shares her personal story of leaving Wall Street in 1998 after an emotional breakdown on church steps, and how a conversation with her father gave her permission to pursue meaningful work. They discuss burnout, the shift from ego-driven ambition to nervous-system-led decision-making, and how to distinguish genuine passion from fear-based choices.

Key Takeaways

  • Use the 'full-body yes or no' principle — if an idea, opportunity, or hire causes dread or tightening in your nervous system, treat it as a hard no regardless of the financial upside.
  • Marie left her Wall Street job on the New York Stock Exchange in 1998 after crying on the steps of Trinity Church mid-workday, realizing that staying meant sacrificing her identity, not just her comfort.
  • Her father's advice — 'you've been working since you were 9, I'm not worried about you; find something you love because you'll be doing it for 40–50 years' — became the permission she needed to quit.
  • Ego-driven ambition is useful early in a career to build belief and take action, but over time it can hijack your decisions and lead to burnout if it overrides your deeper values.
  • 2025 has been a year of transformation for Marie marked by greater ease and play, signaling that high achievers can consciously shift from 'do whatever it takes' hustle to a more sustainable, intuition-led operating mode.
