About the Guest

Alex Mashrabov
Founder & CEO, Higgsfield AI

Alex Mashrabov is the founder and CEO of Higgsfield AI, an AI video and image generation platform that reached $200M in annual recurring revenue within 9 months of launch — a growth rate faster than Slack, Zoom, or Dropbox. Before Higgsfield, Alex accumulated deep experience in consumer technology and product development, which informed his customer-first, rapid-iteration approach to building AI products. He is a vocal advocate for lean founding teams, bootstrapped growth, and treating AI as the next industrial revolution.

In this episode of the Silicon Valley Girl Podcast, Marina Mogilko interviews Alex Mashrabov, Founder & CEO, Higgsfield AI. Marina Mogilko interviews Alex Mashrabov, founder of Higgsfield AI, who shares how his company reached $200M in annual recurring revenue in just 9 months — faster than Slack, Zoom, or Dropbox. Alex breaks down his exact playbook for building a $1M ARR business in 90 days starting with just two people, and explains how daily iteration and eight customer interviews led to the product breakthroughs that drove Higgsfield's explosive growth. The conversation also covers the rapid pace of AI model releases, the rise of AI-native marketing agencies, and why Alex believes AI is the most powerful social elevator of our time.

Key Takeaways

  • A founding team of just two people — one builder and one go-to-market person — is enough to launch and scale an AI startup today, targeting $1 in revenue by day 30 and $1M ARR by day 90.
  • Higgsfield's breakthrough came from only 8 customer interviews: every single interviewee, from Hollywood directors to regional commercial producers, cited the same missing feature — camera control — which the team then shipped in March 2024.
  • The AI industry resets every month, with roughly five leading research labs pushing major model updates each quarter, sometimes twice a month, requiring product teams to substantially rebuild their products around new capabilities continuously.
  • From June to December 2024, AI-native marketing agencies emerged and exploded as a new industry, bypassing traditional tools like Adobe to deliver ad campaigns end-to-end with AI in days, dramatically improving their margin profiles.
  • Alex advises founders to avoid raising VC money early and instead focus on bootstrapping to profitability, arguing that many businesses can scale to tens of millions in revenue profitably with AI without external funding.
